Indian cuisine

Indian cuisine consists of a variety of regional and traditional cuisines native to the Indian subcontinent.
Given the diversity in soil, climate, culture, ethnic groups, and occupations, these cuisines vary substantially and use locally available ingredients.

Japanese Cuisine

Japanese cuisine encompasses the regional and traditional foods of Japan, which have developed through centuries of political, economic, and social changes.
The traditional cuisine of Japan is based on rice with miso soup and other dishes with an emphasis on seasonal ingredients.
